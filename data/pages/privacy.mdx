## Privacy Policy

Our mobile application does not collect any personal information from our users. We do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies. Therefore, we do not share any data with third parties.

We do not store any data on our servers or any other external storage devices. All the data generated by our application is stored locally on the user's device and is not accessible to us.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.
