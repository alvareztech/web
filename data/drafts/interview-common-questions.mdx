---
title: Interview Common Questions
---

1. Tell me a little about yourself.
2. How did you hear about the position?
3. What are you interested in this position?
4. What do you consider your strengths and weaknesses?
5. Tell about a time when you overcame a challenge at work.
6. What are you career goals?
7. Why do you want to work here?
8. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
9. Why do you want to work here?
10. Do you have any questions for me?
